# Visual consistency

Review the approved anchor and every asset at full size, 48 px, and 24 px.

## Technical signals

- identical canvas dimensions and format;
- alpha channel and truly transparent corners;
- visible bounds near the configured safe area;
- centered optical mass without edge contact;
- no corrupt or duplicate files;
- no unexplained luminance or file-size outliers;
- stable filenames and complete mappings.

## Visual signals

- recognizable silhouette at 24 px;
- consistent camera and perspective;
- consistent upper/lower lighting direction;
- consistent material roughness and highlight strength;
- consistent outline and shadow policy;
- comparable visual weight across the set;
- one coherent accent-color rule;
- no text, letters, logos, borders, plates, or accidental extra objects;
- no checkerboards, halos, matte residue, or generated background fragments.

Use the deterministic validator to find candidates, then inspect the contact sheet on both light and dark backgrounds. Technical validation cannot determine whether a symbol communicates the intended meaning.

Regenerate or edit individual outliers. Do not flatten the whole set with global blur, color overlays, or aggressive sharpening merely to make metrics converge.
